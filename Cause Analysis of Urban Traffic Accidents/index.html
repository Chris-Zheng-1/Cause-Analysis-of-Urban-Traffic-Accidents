Cause Analysis of Urban Traffic Accidents

The research on road traffic safety has an important position . Actually, traffic accidents do not only disrupt the traffic order but also cause economic losses and casualties. At present, the visualization format of traffic accident information is mainly in the form of histograms and polylines. With the current high volume of traffic data, traditional visualization models have certain limitations in reflecting the global structure and specific details which are the essential research elements. My study will analysis the British traffic accident data from 2005 to 2015.
