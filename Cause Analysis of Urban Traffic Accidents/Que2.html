Traffic Accidents happened in different road Type

The vast majority of traffic accidents occur in single carriageways, followed by dual carriageways. In the UK, single carriageways are the main component of the highway system, which is why traffic accidents occur on a single carriageway. Besides, the traffic conditions of these two roads are relatively Well, the driver is easy to relax, but on the roundabout and one-way line, the driver can concentrate, which also explains that traffic accidents rarely occur on several other road types.
